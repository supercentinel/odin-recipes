Guacamole

Description

guacamole

Ingredients

  • A avocado
  • 3 chiles serranos
  • A garlic clove
  • Cilantro
  • 1/4 onion
  • The juce of one lemon
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a molcajete grind the garlic clove and the chiles serranos with salt
  2. Add the avocado and smash it
  3. Pour the lemon juice
  4. Chop the cilantro and the onion and add them to the guacamole
  5. Eat
